@President_CyberQuest October 12, 2024

Welcome Our New Mascot! 🦉

After weeks of voting, the student community has chosen the mascot for our newly established club (Founded earlier this year in March 2024)!

Meet cyber_owl_2024! It will accompany us to our CTFs, workshops, and cybersecurity competitions. We chose the owl because it represents wisdom, vigilance, and the night shifts spent studying packet captures and analyzing logs.

@Admin_Sec September 05, 2024

CTF Prep Workshop #2

Thanks to everyone who joined our second practical workshop on Cryptography! We covered Caesar ciphers, Vigenère ciphers, and key transposition methods. The notes have been uploaded to our resources page. Let us know if you want a session on binary exploitation next!

@President_CyberQuest March 15, 2024

CyberQuest Club is Officially Live!

We are officially recognized as a college club starting today! Our goal is to make cybersecurity learning fun, practical, and highly accessible to all departments, not just CS. Stay tuned for our inaugural Capture The Flag event coming later this term.
